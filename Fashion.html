    Styling Tips

  • The majority of the time it's extremely hard for me to find pants that fit properly. Most of the time I have to roll my pants at the ankles, so the lenghth works for me. Even though I probably should start buying petite style pants, but I actually now prefer just to roll the bottoms of my jeans which I think adds even more detail to your outfit. If you do roll your pants, I would say pair it with flat shoes.

  • I read somewhere on the way French women decide on what shoe to wear Withouttheir outfits. And it said that they, wear flat shoes with any garment above/at the knee. And anything below that point they wear heels. Which Makes a lot of sense, if you're wearing a dress that is long, wearing flats could cause the dress to drag across the floor. But, if you wear a short shirt with heels, in my opinion wouldn't make the outfit classy, so if you plan on wearing a shorter bottom, go with flats!

    • Shopping Tips

  • Always Look in the clearance or sale section of a store. You can almost always find something you want, at a reasonable price.
  • Shop at 2nd hand or thrift store. I reccomended stores like this because at stores like this you have the chance to find really unique articles of clothing at a decent price.
  • When it comes to shopping online you should, always sort items from "low to high."
